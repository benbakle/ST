For those times when you are in a time crunch, SensorTec offers expedited delivery options. Just give us a call, 260-497-8811, for express options for your specific part number(s). We will have an answer back to you within one business hour.


Expedite Options:



Express 1:
1-day shipping ARO. If the order is submitted and approved before 12:00 PM (EST) Monday-Friday between 8 AM & 5 PM (EST) it will ship the following business day.

Express 3:
3-day shipping ARO. If the order is submitted and approved before 12:00 PM (EST) Monday-Friday between 8 AM & 5 PM (EST) it will ship in three business days (including the day of order).

Express 5:

5-day shipping ARO. If the order is submitted and approved before 12:00 PM (EST) Monday-Friday between 8 AM & 5 PM (EST) it will ship in five business days (including the day of order).



Considerations:


  • Express orders do not include shipping or handling costs. Please note your preferred shipping method on the purchase order.

  • Express orders must have factory approval and are subject to the availability of parts. If the material(s) is not in stock for an expedited order the expedite time will not start until the day the material arrives at SensorTec.

  • If the expedited item(s) is part of a split order, then the expedited portion must meet minimum order requirements.

  • Verbal express order must be confirmed with a hard purchase order.
